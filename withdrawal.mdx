Synthesis supports withdrawals from **USDC**, **USDC.E** , **USDT** & on **Polygon**, **BSC**, **Ethereum**, **Solana**, **Tron**, **Optimism**, **Arbitrum** & **Base**.

![Withdrawal](/images/Screenshot2025-12-19at1.19.38AM.png)

<Note>
USDC withdrawals on Solana may incur a withdrawal fee to cover rent if the receiving account isn't initialized.
</Note>
