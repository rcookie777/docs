---
title: "Glossary"
description: "Key terms for prediction markets"
---

- **Ask**: Lowest price someone is willing to sell at
- **Bid**: Highest price someone is willing to buy at
- **Spread**: Ask − Bid; smaller is better liquidity
- **Market order**: Executes immediately at the best available prices
- **Limit order**: Executes only at your chosen price or better
- **Stop‑loss**: Triggers a market sell once price hits your stop
- **PnL**: Profit and Loss; unrealized changes with price, realized after closing
- **Resolution**: Final outcome of a market; shares settle to $1.00 or $0.00
- **Liquidity**: How much size can trade without moving price
- **Slippage**: Difference between expected and actual fill price
