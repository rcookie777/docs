## Position sizing

- Start small while learning; scale with confidence
- Avoid risking more than you can afford to lose

## Order selection

- Use **market** only on tight spreads and small size
- Use **limit** for price control or larger orders
- Add **stop‑loss** to cap downside on volatile markets

## Research

- Read resolution criteria carefully; look for edge cases
- Follow reliable news sources; note catalysts and timing

## Discipline

- Predefine exit plans; don’t average down blindly
- Review trades and P&L regularly in your Portfolio
