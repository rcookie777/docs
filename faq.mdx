<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Synthesis?">
    Synthesis is the most advanced prediction market trading interface. We provide tools for discovery, data analysis, and social trading to help you make informed decisions on prediction markets.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I create an account?">
    Visit [synthesis.trade](https://synthesis.trade) and sign in to get started. Your wallet will be automatically created and secured by [Privy](https://privy.io).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I deposit funds?">
    Navigate to your wallet section in the app and follow the deposit instructions. For detailed steps, see our [Deposit Guide](/deposit).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I withdraw funds?">
    Go to your wallet and select the withdrawal option. For detailed steps, see our [Withdrawal Guide](/withdrawal).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I export my wallet?">
    Yes, you can export your wallet for use with other applications. See our [Export Wallet Guide](/export-wallet) for instructions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do rewards work?">
    Synthesis rewards active traders and community contributors. Learn more in our [Rewards Guide](/rewards).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What platforms are supported?">
    Synthesis supports Kalshi on Solana and Polymarket on Polygon.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How can I get support?">
    You can reach our support team at [support@synthesis.trade](mailto:support@synthesis.trade) or join our [Discord community](https://discord.gg/uUdw2KH7H8) and open a ticket!
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
