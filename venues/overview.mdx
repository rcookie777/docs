## Two venues, one app

- **Polymarket**: crypto markets on Polygon (USDC). Global access, low fees, self‑custody.
- **Kalshi**: CFTC‑regulated US exchange (USD). KYC required, US residents only.

## Switching venues

The app automatically routes orders using the correct connected account. You can connect both and trade either venue without leaving the app.
