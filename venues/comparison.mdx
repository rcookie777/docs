| Feature | Polymarket | Kalshi |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Access | Global (crypto) | US residents (regulated) |
| Currency | USDC (Polygon) | USD account |
| Onboarding | Connect wallet | Email + KYC |
| Fees | Lower + Polygon gas | Venue trading fees |
| Custody | Self‑custody | Custodied |
| Order Types | Market, Limit, Stop‑loss | Market, Limit |

<Note>
  You can connect both and choose per‑market; the app routes orders to the correct venue automatically.
</Note>
