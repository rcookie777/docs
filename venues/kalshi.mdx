## Account & funding

- Sign in with email; complete **KYC** (US residents only)
- Link a bank account; deposit via ACH (free), wire (fee), or card (fee)

## Trading specifics

- Order types: Market, Limit (stop‑loss availability may vary by venue)
- Fees: per‑contract trading fees; see Kalshi schedule
- Settlement: proceeds post to your USD account; withdrawals via ACH/wire

<Warning>
  Regulatory restrictions apply; ensure you’re eligible before trading.
</Warning>
