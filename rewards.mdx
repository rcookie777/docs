## How rewards work

- Earn **points** by trading and engaging on the platform
- Progress through **tiers** for enhanced benefits
- See your balance, total earned, and tier progress in your profile under Rewards

## Where to find it

Go to Profile → Rewards. You’ll see:

- Points balance and total points earned
- Your current tier and progress to the next tier
- Your personal referral link

## Claiming and using rewards

- Points accrue automatically as you trade
- Cashbacks/claims may be enabled periodically; watch the Rewards section for availability

<Note>
  Referral commissions and points may be distributed after trades settle; check your Rewards page for updates.
</Note>
