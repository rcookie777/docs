## Timeframes and data

- Switch between multiple timeframes to analyze trends
- Charts reflect historical trades and mid‑prices
- Portfolio view includes a P&L chart over time for each wallet

## Reading the chart

- Rising price → increasing implied probability
- Consolidation near 50¢ often precedes breakouts
- Spikes can occur around news; spreads may widen

<Note>
  In your profile’s Portfolio, the P&L chart aggregates your positions and updates in real time based on live market prices.
</Note>
