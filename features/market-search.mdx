## Quick open

- Press ⌘/Ctrl+K anywhere to open search
- Type keywords; filter by category or venue

## Tips

- Use specific terms (e.g., “BTC 100k 2025”) for faster results
- Toggle venue (Polymarket / Kalshi) when you know where you want to trade
