<aside>
### Click “Deposit” in the top-right.

Once you have signed in, click the deposit button at the top right.
</aside>

<aside>
### Select Token & Chain

If depositing via crypto, select which token you would like to deposit and your respective chain. For example, USDC on the Polygon chain.

Synthesis supports **USDC**, **USDC.E** (Ethereum Bridged), **USDT** & **ETH**.

</aside>

<aside>
### Deposit Crypto

Transfer crypto to your Synthesis account by scanning the QR code or copying your wallet address.

![Deposit](/images/Screenshot 2025-12-19 at 1.01.50 AM.png)

</aside>


To prevent fund loss, transfer crypto assets only over the respective network supported.
