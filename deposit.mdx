---
title: "Deposit"
description: "Fund your Synthesis account"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Deposit' in the top-right.">
    Once you have signed in, click the deposit button at the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Token & Chain">
    If depositing via crypto, select which token you would like to deposit and your respective chain. For example, USDC on the Polygon chain.

    Synthesis supports depostings **USDC**, **USDC.E** , **USDT** & **ETH** on **Polygon**, **BSC**, **Ethereum**, **Solana**, **Tron**, **Optimism**, **Arbitrum** & **Base**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deposit Crypto">
    Transfer crypto to your Synthesis account by scanning the QR code or copying your deposit wallet address.

    ![Deposit](/images/Screenshot%202025-12-19%20at%201.01.50%20AM.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>


To prevent fund loss, transfer crypto assets only over the respective network supported.
